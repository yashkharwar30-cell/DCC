Core Subjects & WeightageThe Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE) follows a specific curriculum for the Class 10 (SSC) Board Exams.1. Languages (First, Second, and Third Language)English (Higher or Lower Level): Focuses on Prose, Poetry, Writing Skills (Letter Writing, Report Writing, Essay), and Grammar.Marathi/Hindi/Sanskrit: Comprehensive study of literature, functional grammar, and creative writing.2. MathematicsAlgebra (Maths I): Linear Equations in Two Variables, Quadratic Equations, Arithmetic Progression, Financial Planning, Probability, and Statistics.Geometry (Maths II): Similarity, Pythagoras Theorem, Circle, Geometric Constructions, Co-ordinate Geometry, Trigonometry, and Mensuration.3. Science and TechnologyScience Part-1: Principles of Gravitation, Periodic Classification of Elements, Chemical Reactions, Effects of Electric Current, Heat, Refraction of Light, Lenses, Metallurgy, and Space Missions.Science Part-2: Heredity and Evolution, Life Processes, Environmental Management, Animal Classification, Introduction to Microbiology, Cell Biology, and Social Health.4. Social SciencesHistory & Political Science: Applied History, Mass Media and History, Entertainment and History, Indian Constitution, and Electoral Process.Geography: Detailed comparative study between India and Brazil, covering Physiography, Climate, Economy, and Population.Internal Assessment & Practical ExamsIn addition to the written board papers, students are evaluated on:Science Practicals: Journal completion and hands-on experiments.Maths Internal: Home assignments and MCQ-based unit tests.Oral Exams: For all languages (Listening and Speaking skills).Exam Pattern HighlightsSubjectWritten MarksInternal MarksTotalLanguages8020100Mathematics80 (40+40)20100Science80 (40+40)20100Social Science80 (40+40)20100
