We provide a dual-focus approach: mastering the Maharashtra Board syllabus while building the foundation for CET/JEE/NEET.

1. Physics (Conceptual & Numerical Mastery)
Rotational & Fluid Dynamics: Angular momentum, Torque, Surface Tension, and Bernoulli’s Principle.

Thermodynamics: Laws of Thermodynamics, Heat Engines, and Refrigerators.

Oscillations & Waves: Simple Harmonic Motion (SHM), Superposition of Waves, and Doppler Effect.

Optics: Wave Theory of Light, Interference, and Diffraction.

Electromagnetism: Electrostatics, Capacitors, Current Electricity (Kirchhoff’s Laws), and Magnetic Fields.

Modern Physics: Bohr’s Atomic Model, Photoelectric Effect, Nuclear Fission/Fusion, and Semiconductor Logic Gates.

2. Chemistry (The Science of Matter)
Physical Chemistry: Solid State (Crystal lattices), Solutions (Raoult's Law), Chemical Thermodynamics, and Electrochemistry (Nernst Equation).

Inorganic Chemistry: Extraction of Elements, P-Block, D-Block, and Coordination Compounds (Ligands/Isomerism).

Organic Chemistry: Reaction mechanisms of Halogen Derivatives, Alcohols, Phenols, Aldehydes, and Ketones.

Biomolecules & Polymers: Study of Carbohydrates, Proteins, DNA/RNA, and Synthetic Polymers (Nylon, Polythene).

Green Chemistry: Principles of sustainable chemistry and Nanochemistry.

3. Biology (The Living World)
Plant Sciences: Reproduction in Plants, Photosynthesis (Calvin Cycle), and Respiration (Krebs Cycle).

Human Anatomy & Physiology: Circulation, Excretion, Control and Coordination (Nervous System), and Chemical Coordination (Hormones).

Genetics & Evolution: Mendelian Inheritance, DNA Replication, Protein Synthesis, and Human Evolution.

Biotechnology: Genetic Engineering, PCR, and Applications in Medicine/Agriculture.

4. Mathematics & Statistics
Part 1: Mathematical Logic, Matrices, Trigonometric Functions, Pair of Straight Lines, Vectors, and 3D Geometry.

Part 2: Limits, Continuity, Differentiation, Integration (Definite/Indefinite), Differential Equations, and Binomial Distribution.
