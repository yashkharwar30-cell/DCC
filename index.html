About Us

About Us – Dhruv Coaching Classes

DHRUV COACHING CLASSES is a "Centre of Excellence" dedicated to helping students achieve academic success through quality teaching and proper guidance. Established in 2003 , the institute is known as a trusted coaching center for SSC, CBSE, IGCSE, and HSC (Commerce & Science) students.

We focus on clear concepts, regular practice, and personal attention to every student. Our experienced teachers ensure simple and effective learning, supported by tests and performance tracking.

With a disciplined environment and modern facilities, Dhruv Coaching Classes aims to build strong foundations and help students achieve their best results.
